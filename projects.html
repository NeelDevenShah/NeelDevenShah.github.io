MediPriority

Leveraging advanced AI technologies, MediPriority aims to improve patient outcomes, enhance efficiency, and reduce the administrative burden on healthcare providers. Key features include multi-agent AI systems for various tasks, dynamic urgency scoring, visual analysis, personalized AI avatars, and seamless integration with everyday tools like Google Calendar.

Amazon ML Hackathon 2024

In the 2024 Amazon ML Hackathon, our team achieved an impressive rank of 64th out of over 75,000 participants from across India. Our project focused on Feature Extraction from Images, utilizing advanced machine learning techniques to extract critical product information directly from images. This capability is essential in fields such as healthcare and e-commerce. Our solution leverages the MiniCPM-Llama3-V-2.5 model, a cutting-edge vision-language model that integrates visual processing capabilities with natural language understanding.

Warehouse Autobot

Dosepacker Inc. runs a large automated warehouse, where robots (called autobots) move products from one place to another. However, the system recently started facing issues like traffic jams and collisions, slowing down operations. Our goal was to create a smart system to control these autobots, helping them move safely and efficiently within the warehouse without bumping into each other or obstacles. Our mission was to guide Autobots across a matrix grid from their starting point (A) to their destination (B), avoiding obstacles and each other.
